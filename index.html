About Us

Christian-Based Non-Governmental Organization

At The Genuine Women's Foundation, our mission is to empower women and girls through mentorship, leadership development, and mental health support, preparing them to realize their full potential and thrive in both personal and professional spaces. We are focused on putting a smile on women through teachings, guidance, and empowerment.

Our Mission
  • Provide educational programs
  • Promote peace and professional friendship among women
  • Empower women to lead healthy, peaceful, and fulfilling lives
Why We Exist?

The Genuine Women's Foundation exists to address the challenges women face, providing them with the education, skills, and encouragement needed to overcome obstacles, aiming to inspire and uplift women to lead fulfilling lives.

Prof. Olufunke Rebecca Vincent

Founder

Professor Olufunke Rebecca Vincent is a Nigerian contemporary leader in Artificial Intelligence and an Evangelist. She did her PhD field work in Germany and also a Post-Doctoral Fellowship of the TWAS-DFG Cooperation Visits Programme for Scientists from Sub-Saharan Africa at the Technical University of Clausthal, (TU-Clausthal), Germany. She had visited several international institutions for study, training, workshops, conferences, and research in Germany, the United Kingdom, India, and Italy

She has won national and international recognition and awards. She has won a good number of national and international recognitions and awards, including the Federal Government award for postgraduate students in 2003, Ph.D. Research Fellow in 2008, a Post-Doctoral Fellowship award in 2014, and a Google Scholar Award in Ranking Web of Universities in Cybermetrics Laboratory, Spain in 2015. In January 2024, the University's AD Scientific Index Rankings of Scientists ranked Prof. Olufunke Vincent as one of the first fifty top researchers in Computer Science in Nigeria, 2024. In February 2024, She won the prestigious Ekiti Future Agenda Award and Meritorious Service Award in January, 2025 by Premier Grammar School Alumni.

A fellow of the Nigeria Computer Society and a lecturer at the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta (FUNAAB), she is the present Head of Department of Cbersecurity and Data Science. She is a member of the Computer Professional Registration Council of Nigeria (CPN), the Vice-President of Academia in Information Technology Profession (AITP), and a member of Nigeria Women in Information Technology (Niwiit). She is happily married with Children.

However, the hand of the Lord has been mighty upon her to start using her experiences for mentoring. She believes a woman should be more fortified with divine wisdom from the proper knowledge and information to make waves in our journey on earth. Her book, Divine Intelligence - Unlocking the Secrets of Intimacy with God can be gotten here.
